## Quick Reference

### Step Duration Summary
```
Step 1: Welcome              ~3 min
Step 2: Social Connection    ~3-5 min (optional, can skip)
Step 3: Persona Intro        ~5 min (reading/learning)
Step 4: Interview            ~5-10 min (user input)
Step 5: Posts Upload         ~2-5 min (user input)
Step 6: AI Generation        ~1 min (automatic)
Step 7: Review Persona       ~1-2 min (reading)
Step 8: Flows Setup          ~5 min (reading/learning)
Step 9: Completion          ~1 min (celebration)
                             ─────────────────
                             ~26-32 minutes total

If user skips social connection:
                             ~23-27 minutes total
```

## Decision Points

```
Start at /guided-onboarding
    │
    ├─ Click "Maybe Later" ──→ /dashboard (skip onboarding)
    │
    └─ Click "Let's Get Started" ──→ Step 2
        │
        ├─ Step 2: Skip social ──→ Step 3
        │
        └─ Step 2: Connect social ──→ OAuth ──→ Step 3
            │
            ├─ Step 3: Click back ──→ Step 2
            │
            └─ Step 3: Continue ──→ Step 4
                │
                ├─ Step 5: Skip ──→ Step 6
                │
                └─ Step 5: Upload ──→ Step 6 ──→ Step 7
                    │
                    └─ Step 9: Go to Dashboard ──→ /dashboard
```

## Key Touchpoints

1. **Welcome Screen** - Set expectations and excitement
2. **Social Connection** - Make it optional and educational
3. **Persona Intro** - Educate about what they're creating
4. **Interview** - Gather actual user input
5. **Posts Upload** - Use existing content or manual entry
6. **AI Generation** - Show that work is happening
7. **Confirmation** - Show the impressive results
8. **Flows Intro** - Excite about automation
9. **Success** - Celebrate and move forward

Each step reinforces why the user is doing it and what happens next.
