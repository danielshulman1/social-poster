   → Create app → Get App ID & Secret

   → pnpm dev

   → Click: "Connect with Facebook"
   → Select: Your page

⚠️ COMMON ISSUES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

❌ "Connect with Facebook" button does nothing
   ✅ Solution: Add FACEBOOK_APP_ID to .env.local and restart server

❌ "No pages found" after login
   ✅ Solution: Make sure you selected pages in Facebook permissions popup

❌ Instagram account doesn't appear
   ✅ Solution: Verify Instagram is Business Account and linked to Facebook Page

❌ "Invalid OAuth Redirect URI"
   ✅ Solution: Check redirect URI in Facebook App matches: 
      http://localhost:3000/api/auth/facebook/callback

See FACEBOOK_INSTAGRAM_SETUP.md for full troubleshooting.
